Why Vitamins?

Many North Americans have some sort of vitamin deficiency. Unfortunately, many are unaware of their condition. Even those who do supplement their diet with pills often take excessive amounts or not enough. Both low and high vitamin levels have been linked to diseases.

We often watch our diet, take pills, or eat foods fortified with vitamins. Yet we never know what is actually being absorbed, or how much our bodies actually need. Interactions from what you eat or drink may affect the absorption of vitamins. The only way to know if you are healthy is to get tested regularly.

What Do We Have To Offer?

Understanding your vitamin levels and vitamin needs has always been an expensive and time-consuming task. Our product will enable those with related medical conditions, pregnant women, athletes—and everyone generally concerned with their health—the ability to understand their body's vitamin levels.

With experience in nanotechnology and bio-sensors our team has created the Vitameter: a handheld device that measures your vitamin levels within minutes. Each test is inexpensive and provides you with the information needed to manage your diet and supplements.
